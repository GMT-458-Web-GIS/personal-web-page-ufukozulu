My Homeland

I was born in Çorum in 1999. Çorum is a city and province located in the Black Sea Region of Turkey. According to 2023 data, the population of Çorum is approximately 525,000 people. The city is known for its historical richness, particularly the remains of the Hittite civilization. Its economy is based on agriculture and animal husbandry. Çorum is also famous for its delicious İskilip dolması and roasted chickpeas (leblebi). With its natural beauty and cultural heritage, it attracts both domestic and foreign tourists.
